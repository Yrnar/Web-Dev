Recipe #1

  • Time: 15min
  • Serves: Deli books: about breakfast
Ingredients Quantity
Egg 2p
Milk 120ml
Vegetable oil 1tp
Butter 5g
Salt, ground pepper As you wish

Step 1: Eggs for an omelet in a frying pan, shake with light movements of a fork with milk in a small bowl. Do not whisk, namely shake – just to mix the protein and yolk.


Step 2: Place a frying pan with a diameter of about 20 cm on the stove to make an omelet. Turn on medium heat and let the container warm up (it will take about a minute). Add both types of oil and turn the pan so that it is distributed over the entire surface. Increase the heat to maximum.


Step 3: When the butter starts to foam, pour in the egg mixture with milk. Tilting the pan in different directions, let it spread evenly. Put it on the fire and count down 6 seconds.


Step 4: Tilt the pan at an angle of 45 °, pick up the gripped edge of the omelet with a spatula and slide it to the middle (the liquid part from the center will drain into its place). Do the same with the opposite edge.


Step 5: Repeat this operation a couple more times to grab the entire surface of the omelet with milk (it only takes about 40 seconds). Season with salt and pepper and serve.
